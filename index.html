Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of the communication among Chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap arounf tree branches

Interesting facts about Bears

  1. Bears are an extraordinarily intelligent species
  2. Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
  3. The largest mammalian carnivore (meat eater) that ever lived on land was the giant short-faced bear

    Interesting facts about Pandas

    2. Pandas can easily camouflage in their environment
